What We Do

Key Divisions

01

Event Management

  • We offer full Event Management
  • From Registration to parting gifts and everything in between. We create a seamless brand experience that exceeds expectations.
  • Our proprietary event management system ensures that we execute every element of your event with precision resulting in memorable events that deliver value to you and your brand.
02

Event Bar & Catering

  • Food Choices at an event are so much more than just food.
  • Our in-house team creates a food strategy that ensures that your guests are not only getting a scrumptious gourmet experience but also connect with the brand through tasteful execution that is on the brand message.
  • We can create any food experience that you can imagine.
03

Décor Elements

  • We specialize in using décor to stimulate senses and leave a lasting impression.
  • Our Décor experience includes pre-event décor setup so that you can experience that event before it is executed.
  • We maintain an extensive décor repository to ensure that we create an experience that matches your brand equity.
04

Content & Design

  • We generate a content execution strategy that allows your message to shine.
  • Our team includes Creative Directors, Graphic Designers, Art Directors and Copywriters who execute your message so that there is greater message comprehension.
  • We also can incorporate the latest technology into your event to ensure your brand look and feel is top-of-mind for attendees.
05

Entertainment

  • Our entertainment division has access to some of the biggest artists in South Africa.
  • If required, we can source any international artists for your event.
  • Our entertainment services extend from MC's to music and full entertainment creation services.
06

Technical Gear

  • Our technical division will supply the best technical equipment to your event.
  • From small events to stadium events we maintain a registry of the latest technical equipment for a seamless execution
  • Ground-breaking and latest in staging and screens to provide the best set up for your event.
07

Public Relations & Marketing

  • We maintain a close relationship with media publications as this enables us to provide the best in PR and Marketing for your brand.
  • We generate public relations initiatives that ensure awareness of your launch, activity or product.
  • Our experienced team of PR & Marketing professionals can take messages to the media within a day of briefing for maximum exposure.
08

Incentive Travel

  • We organise and manage incentive packages for corporates for their staff and executives.
  • This is includes and is not limited to: All travel and accommodation bookings, conferencing, executive meetings, gifting, complete itineraries, etc.
  • We do both national and international travel for incentive packages.
09

Promotions & Activations

  • With our large team of experienced and trained Promotional staff / Brand ambassadors we can create activations that excite and inform. From registration, welcomes, trophy ladies to activation staff.
  • We conceptualize and execute activations based on brand strategy.
  • We can supply all the elements for the activation to ensure that all you focus on is the results.
